### Full Content
## Preparation

Congratulations on reaching this milestone! Here's what you need to know as you prepare for commencement.

### Regalia

All graduates are required to wear academic regalia during the ceremony. Regalia can be ordered through the CCA Bookstore. Orders must be placed by **April 15, 2026** to ensure timely delivery.

- Undergraduate regalia includes a cap, gown, and tassel
- Graduate regalia includes a cap, gown, hood, and tassel
- Regalia pickup will be available at the bookstore starting **May 1, 2026**

### Ceremony Day Checklist

- Arrive at least **90 minutes** before your ceremony start time
- Check in at the registration desk to receive your name card
- Bring your regalia — there will be a robing area on-site
- Leave valuables at home or with a guest; storage is limited backstage
- Guests are not permitted in the staging area

### Rehearsal

A brief walkthrough will take place in the staging area prior to each ceremony. Detailed instructions will be emailed to graduating students one week before commencement.

### Name Pronunciation

To ensure your name is pronounced correctly during the ceremony, you will have the opportunity to submit a phonetic spelling or audio recording. Details will be sent via email in April.

### Diploma Information

Diplomas are not distributed at the ceremony. Official diplomas will be mailed to graduates approximately 8–10 weeks after commencement, once all degree requirements have been verified by the Registrar's Office.

### Accessibility

If you require accommodations, please contact the Office of Accessibility at accessibility@cca.edu at least two weeks before the ceremony. Accessible seating, sign language interpretation, and other services are available upon request.

### Photography

Professional photographers will be on-site — individual portraits will be available for purchase after the event. A link to the photo gallery will be emailed to all graduates within two weeks of the ceremony.

### Social Media

Share your commencement experience using **#CCA2026**. Tag us on Instagram at @caaboratory.

## Commencement — For Families & Friends

### Full Content
## Attending Commencement

We're delighted to welcome you to campus to celebrate your graduate's achievement.

### Tickets

Each graduate will receive **four guest tickets**. Additional tickets may become available closer to the event. Tickets are required for entry and are non-transferable. Ticket distribution details will be shared with graduates in April.

### Transportation & Parking

Yerba Buena Center for the Arts is located at 701 Mission St, San Francisco. The venue is accessible via BART (Powell St or Montgomery St stations) and Muni. Limited street parking is available; we recommend public transit.

- **BART**: Exit at Powell St or Montgomery St station (5-minute walk)
- **Muni**: Lines 14, 30, and 45 stop within one block
- **Parking**: Nearby garages include the 5th & Mission Garage and Moscone Center Garage

### Accessibility for Guests

The venue is fully ADA accessible, including wheelchair seating, elevators, and accessible restrooms. If you need specific accommodations, please contact commencement@cca.edu in advance.

### Livestream

Can't attend in person? Both ceremonies will be livestreamed on this website. Links will be posted the week of commencement. Recorded video will remain available after the event.

### What to Expect

Each ceremony is approximately 90 minutes. Doors open 60 minutes before the start time. The ceremony includes a processional, welcome remarks, student and alumni speakers, the conferral of degrees, and a recessional.

### Campus Map & Nearby Amenities

A campus map and list of nearby restaurants and amenities will be shared closer to the event. The Yerba Buena neighborhood offers many dining and sightseeing options within walking distance.

### Photography Policy

Guests are welcome to take photos from their seats. Please remain seated during the ceremony and avoid blocking the view of other guests. Professional photos will be available online after the event.

### Reception

Light refreshments will be available in the lobby after each ceremony. Graduates will join their guests in the reception area following the recessional.
